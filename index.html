About IndusRoboTix

Building Pakistan's Robotics Future

Founded in 2026 by Furqan Khatti, IndusRoboTix was born from a passion for robotics and a vision to make robotic technology accessible in Pakistan.

We specialize in assembling custom robotic kits using locally sourced components combined with our precision-designed acrylic chassis. This approach allows us to provide affordable, high-quality robotics solutions tailored to the needs of Pakistani students, hobbyists, and professionals.

Our mission is to advance STEM education and innovation in Pakistan by providing reliable, locally-supported robotics technology that empowers the next generation of engineers and innovators.
